Enduring Values Across 150 Years

⛏️

Free Enterprise & Opportunity

From the Gold Rush prospectors who risked everything to the entrepreneurs who built Cherry Creek Shopping Center, free enterprise and individual initiative have driven Cherry Creek's prosperity for 150 years.

🏠

Faith, Family & Community

Churches, tight-knit families, and neighbor helping neighbor built Cherry Creek from the ground up. Through floods, depressions, and wars, strong families and faith communities held the area together.

🌊

Taming the Frontier

Cherry Creek's pioneers didn't wait for government — they built homes, businesses, and infrastructure through grit and determination. When floods threatened, they engineered solutions like the Cherry Creek Dam.

🛍️

Commerce & Prosperity

Private investment — not government programs — transformed Cherry Creek into a premier destination. From the 1953 shopping center to today's luxury district, the free market built this community.

🇺🇸

Patriotism & Service

Cherry Creek answered every call to serve — from WWI victory gardens to WWII war bonds to supporting Lowry and Buckley military bases. Love of country runs deep in this community.

📈

Growth Through Limited Government

Colorado's low-tax, business-friendly environment attracted waves of entrepreneurs and families. Cherry Creek's success story is proof that economic freedom creates thriving communities.
